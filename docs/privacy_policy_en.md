# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

## 1. App and contact

This Privacy Policy applies to the `PaperBell` app.

- Global app name: PaperBell
- Recommended default Play title: PaperBell - Doc Reminder
- Korean localized listing title: 담아알림 - 문서 리마인더
- Contact email: `lup53699@gmail.com`

PaperBell is a local-first app that helps organize reminders from imported documents such as images, PDFs, notices, and other everyday paperwork.

## 2. Core approach

PaperBell is built around the following principles:

- No login
- No dedicated backend server
- No authentication system
- No cloud sync
- Core document and reminder data stays on the device

## 3. Data handled by the app

The app handles documents imported by the user and the reminder information derived from those documents on the device.

Examples include:

- imported image files
- imported PDF files
- extracted text and parsing results from OCR or PDF reading
- titles, dates, amounts, notes, and reminder details that the user edits or saves

This data is stored on the device by default and is not sent to the app's own servers.

## 4. What the app does not do

PaperBell does not currently operate by:

- creating user accounts
- requiring login
- sending documents or extracted results to its own backend
- providing cloud backup or cloud sync
- collecting personal data through advertising networks

## 5. Donation purchases

The app may include optional one-time support purchases.

- donation purchases may be processed through Google Play or Apple App Store billing systems
- the app itself does not directly store full payment card details
- payment processing, receipts, and transaction retention may follow the policies of the relevant app store provider

## 6. Contact email and voluntary communication

If you send an email inquiry, the information you choose to include in that email is transmitted through email.

This means:

- it is separate from in-app local data handling
- the developer may receive and read the information you voluntarily send

Please send only what is necessary and be careful with sensitive personal information.

## 7. Retention and deletion

In-app data may remain on the device until:

- you delete the relevant data in the app
- you uninstall the app
- it remains within your own device environment under device backup or operating system behavior

Deletion options:

- you can delete reminders or local app data within the app
- uninstalling the app may remove its local data from the device

However, app store billing records and emails you send may be retained separately under the policies of those service providers.

## 8. User caution

Documents you import may contain personal information, such as:

- name
- address
- payment details
- contract details

For that reason, basic device security such as screen lock, backup control, and careful screen sharing is strongly recommended.

## 9. Changes

This document may be updated if the app's features or data handling change.

When important changes are made, related store information and in-app guidance should also be reviewed together.
